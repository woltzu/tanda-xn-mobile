## 5. Savings circles — how they work

- A savings circle has an amount per cycle, a cycle length (usually
  weekly or monthly), a fixed number of members, and a payout order.
- Each cycle, every member contributes the agreed amount. One member
  receives the full pot at the end of the cycle. The order continues
  until every member has been paid, at which point the circle is
  complete.
- **By joining a circle, you commit to contributing for every cycle
  until the circle completes, whether or not you have already been
  paid out.** Backing out early after receiving a payout is a breach
  of these Terms and may result in negative reporting to your XnScore,
  suspension of your account, or referral to collection.
- If you miss a contribution, the "Partial Contribution" and
  "Substitution" mechanisms in the app may allow you to catch up or
  temporarily hand your slot to another member. Whether these apply
  depends on your circle's settings.
- Circle members may vote to modify circle rules under the governance
  mechanisms provided in the app. Votes are binding on all members.
