[ENTITY] ("we", "us", or "TandaXn") operates the TandaXn mobile

## 2. Information we collect

We collect personal data in three broad categories:

### 2.1 Identity data (provided by you)

- Full legal name, date of birth, government-issued ID number.
- Selfie and document images submitted during KYC.
- Address, phone number, email.
- Tax identification information where required by law.

### 2.2 Financial data (provided by you or generated by your use)

- Linked bank account or debit card details (tokenized through our
  payment partners -- we never store raw card numbers).
- Contribution, payout, advance, and repayment history.
- Wallet balance and transaction history.
- Insurance pool and reputation scores associated with circles you join.

### 2.3 Behavioral data (generated automatically)

- Login times and devices, IP address and approximate location.
- In-app actions: which screens you view, which features you use, how
  long sessions last.
- Communications you send through the in-app chat.
- Performance and crash diagnostics from your device.
- AI-decision metadata such as your XnScore and the underlying factors.

We collect only what is necessary to provide the Service, comply with
law (especially anti-money-laundering ("AML") and counter-terrorism-
financing ("CTF") obligations), and improve the product.

## 3. How we use your information

We process your data for the following purposes:

- **Service delivery.** Operate circles, goals, advances, KYC,
  payments, and the XnScore calculation.
- **Compliance.** AML/CTF screening, sanctions checks, fraud
  detection, regulatory reporting.
- **Security.** Detect and prevent unauthorized account access,
  account-takeover attempts, and suspicious transactions.
- **Communications.** Account notifications, transaction
  confirmations, and (with separate consent) marketing.
- **Improvement.** Aggregate, anonymized analytics to improve
  product performance and user experience.
- **Legal.** Resolve disputes, enforce our Terms, and respond to
  lawful requests.

Our lawful bases (where the GDPR or similar laws apply) include
performance of a contract (delivering the Service), legal obligation
(KYC/AML), and our legitimate interests (fraud prevention, product
improvement), each balanced against your rights.

## 7. Your rights

Subject to applicable law and verification of your identity, you have
the right to:

- **Access** -- request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.
- **Rectification** -- ask us to correct inaccurate or incomplete data.
- **Deletion** -- ask us to erase your data (subject to AML/CTF
  retention requirements).
- **Restriction** -- ask us to limit processing in specific cases.
- **Portability** -- request a machine-readable export of data you
  provided to us.
- **Object** -- object to processing based on legitimate interests.
- **Withdraw consent** -- where processing is based on consent.
- **Lodge a complaint** with your local data-protection authority.

To exercise these rights, contact us at the address in Section 13. We
will respond within the period required by applicable law (typically
30 days).

If you are a California resident, the California Consumer Privacy Act

what categories of personal information we have collected, the right
to delete, and the right to non-discrimination for exercising your
rights.

## 8. Security

We implement technical and organizational measures to protect your
data, including:

- Encryption in transit (TLS 1.3) and at rest (AES-256).
- Tokenization of payment credentials by payment-partner platforms.
- Role-based access controls and audit logging.
- Multi-factor authentication for production access.
- Regular vulnerability scanning and patching.

No security system is perfect. If we become aware of a breach
affecting your personal data, we will notify you and the relevant
authority where required by law.

## 9. Cookies and similar technologies

The mobile application uses local storage and the equivalent of
cookies for session management, fraud detection, and analytics. You
can clear local storage by signing out and clearing app data; this
may disable some features.

## 10. Children's privacy

The Service is not directed to children under 18 years old. We do not
knowingly collect personal data from children. If you believe we have
collected data from a child, contact us in Section 13 and we will
delete it.

## 11. Automated decision-making

screen, and you may request human review by contacting us. You will
not be subject solely to automated decisions producing legal effects
without an opportunity for human review where required by law.
