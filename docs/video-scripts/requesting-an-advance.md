# Tutorial: Requesting an advance

**Runtime:** 80 seconds (target ~195 spoken words)
**Goal:** Eligible user can navigate to the Advance Hub, choose a tier,
review fees + repay-by date, and confirm. Viewer understands the
advance is repaid automatically out of the next circle payout.

---

## Scene 1 -- Hook (0:00 -- 0:08)

- **Scene:** Phone shows the Advance Hub tile on the dashboard. Finger
  taps it.
- **Narration:** "Sometimes you need cash before your payout. Advances
  bridge that gap."
- **On-screen:** `Need funds before your payout?`

## Scene 2 -- Pick a tier (0:08 -- 0:28)

- **Scene:** Advance Hub. Three tier cards: Quick (small, fastest),
  Flex (larger, higher XnScore), Bridge (cross-circle). Each card
  shows the live fee % and the cap. Finger taps Flex.
- **Narration:** "Pick a tier. Quick is small and fast. Flex is
  larger and needs a higher XnScore. Bridge pulls liquidity across
  multiple circles. Each card shows the live fee and your cap."
- **On-screen:** `Quick / Flex / Bridge`

## Scene 3 -- Enter amount + see the math (0:28 -- 0:50)

- **Scene:** Amount input with a slider. As the slider moves, the
  card on the right updates: requested, fee at 30-day, fee at 60-day,
  net received, repay-by date. The fees come from the live pool, not
  a hardcoded number.
- **Narration:** "Enter how much you need. The screen shows your
  fee for 30-day or 60-day repayment, what you'll actually receive,
  and the date the advance comes out of your next payout."
- **On-screen:** `Live fees. No surprises at approval.`

## Scene 4 -- Review + confirm (0:50 -- 1:05)

- **Scene:** Terms screen with checkboxes for fee acknowledgement and
  automatic-offset agreement. Finger ticks both, taps Confirm.
- **Narration:** "Review the terms. Advances are repaid automatically
  from your upcoming payout, so there's no separate bill to track."
- **On-screen:** `Repaid from payout. Nothing to chase.`

## Scene 5 -- Funds arrive (1:05 -- 1:18)

- **Scene:** Wallet screen with the new balance line item, plus a
  notification: "Advance disbursed. $X in your wallet." Cuts to the
  Advance History screen showing the repayment line for the upcoming
  payout date.
- **Narration:** "Approved advances disburse to your wallet within
  minutes. You can watch the repayment progress on Advance History."
- **On-screen:** `Disbursed -> Wallet -> Track in History`

## End CTA (1:18 -- 1:20)

- **Scene:** End card.
- **On-screen:**
  ```
  Eligible? Advance Hub is on your dashboard.
  ```
