# Requesting an Advance
**Runtime:** 80 seconds

## Scene 1: Hook (0:00 – 0:08)
**Visuals:** Phone shows the Advance Hub tile on the dashboard. A small chip below reads "Live fees". Finger taps the tile.
**Narration:** "Sometimes you need cash before your payout. Advances bridge that gap."
**On-screen:** `Need funds before your payout?`

## Scene 2: See your tier and product choice (0:08 – 0:28)
**Visuals:** Advance Hub shows the user's current eligibility tier (e.g., "Standard") with the cap visible. Below it, product cards: Quick Advance, Education Loan, Business Loan, Vehicle Loan. The Quick Advance card is highlighted as the right fit for a short-term need; finger taps it.
**Narration:** "Your eligibility tier — Basic through Elite — sets your cap. Pick a product. Quick Advance is small and fast; the loan products are larger and tied to a specific use."
**On-screen:** `Tier: Standard · Quick Advance: up to $1,000`

## Scene 3: Enter amount, see live fees (0:28 – 0:50)
**Visuals:** Amount input with a slider. As the slider moves, the card on the right updates live: requested amount, 30-day fee %, 60-day fee %, net received, repay-by date. A subtle pulse on the fee number shows the value came from the pool, not a constant.
**Narration:** "Enter how much you need. The screen shows your fee for 30-day or 60-day repayment from the live pool, what you'll actually receive, and the date the advance comes out of your next payout."
**On-screen:** `Live fees. No surprises at approval.`

## Scene 4: Review terms and confirm (0:50 – 1:05)
**Visuals:** Terms screen with two checkboxes: fee acknowledgement and automatic-offset agreement. Finger ticks both, taps Confirm.
**Narration:** "Review the terms. Advances are repaid automatically from your upcoming payout, so there's no separate bill to track."
**On-screen:** `Repaid from payout. Nothing to chase.`

## Scene 5: Disbursement (1:05 – 1:18)
**Visuals:** Wallet screen with the new balance line item, plus a push notification: "Advance disbursed. $750 in your wallet." Cuts to the Advance History screen showing the repayment line for the upcoming payout date.
**Narration:** "Approved advances disburse to your wallet within minutes. Watch repayment progress in Advance History."
**On-screen:** `Disbursed → Wallet → Tracked in History`

## End Card
**Visuals:** TandaXn logo, App Store + Google Play badges.
**Narration:** "Ready to access funds when you need them? Download TandaXn and start today."
**On-screen:** `Eligible? Advance Hub is on your dashboard.`
