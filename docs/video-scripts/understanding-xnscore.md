# Understanding XnScore
**Runtime:** 85 seconds

## Scene 1: Hook (0:00 – 0:10)
**Visuals:** Phone shows the dashboard. A circular score ring sits at 72 / 100 in the XnScore card. Finger taps the card; XnScore Dashboard opens.
**Narration:** "Your XnScore is your TandaXn track record on a zero to one hundred scale. It unlocks bigger circles, better advance terms, and lower insurance rates."
**On-screen:** `XnScore — your TandaXn track record`

## Scene 2: The six factors (0:10 – 0:42)
**Visuals:** XnScore Dashboard. Six bars / dial segments labelled with their weight:
  Payment History 35 pts, Circle Completion 25 pts, Tenure 10 pts, Vouches 10 pts, Defaults -10 pts, Engagement 3 pts.
**Narration:** "Six factors drive it. Payment history is the heaviest — on-time contributions matter most. Circle completion rewards finishing what you start. Tenure builds over time. Vouches reflect who trusts you and whether they completed their circles. Defaults are the negative side. Engagement is the smallest piece."
**On-screen callouts:** `35 Payments` · `25 Completion` · `10 Tenure` · `10 Vouches` · `-10 Defaults` · `3 Engagement`

## Scene 3: The tiers (0:42 – 0:58)
**Visuals:** Tier ladder graphic: Emerging (0-39), Building (40-59), Trusted (60-74), Established (75-89), Elder (90-100). The user's current tier (Trusted) is highlighted.
**Narration:** "Five tiers from Emerging to Elder. Each tier unlocks more — larger advance caps, lower fees, more circles you can join."
**On-screen:** `Emerging → Building → Trusted → Established → Elder`

## Scene 4: Where to see it and improve it (0:58 – 1:18)
**Visuals:** "Top improvement" card surfaces on the dashboard: "Make 2 on-time contributions to gain +3 XnScore." A tap-target jumps straight to the upcoming contributions screen.
**Narration:** "Find your score on the dashboard, and the Top Improvement card tells you the single highest-value action you can take right now. Tap it to jump straight to the screen that resolves it."
**On-screen:** `One tap to your fastest gain`

## Scene 5: Every change is explained (1:18 – 1:28)
**Visuals:** Brief flash of a "Score dropped" notification: "Late contribution: -2 XnScore." Cuts to Decision History with the localized explanation visible.
**Narration:** "Every score change shows up in your Decision History with a plain-language explanation, in your language."
**On-screen:** `Every change. Explained.`

## End Card
**Visuals:** TandaXn logo, App Store + Google Play badges.
**Narration:** "Ready to build your track record? Download TandaXn and start today."
**On-screen:** `Dashboard → XnScore card.`
