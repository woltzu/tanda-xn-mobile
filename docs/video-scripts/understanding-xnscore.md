# Tutorial: Understanding XnScore

**Runtime:** 85 seconds (target ~205 spoken words)
**Goal:** Viewer can read their XnScore, recognize the five factors
that move it, and act on the top improvement opportunity.

---

## Scene 1 -- Hook (0:00 -- 0:10)

- **Scene:** Phone shows the dashboard with a circular score ring at
  72 / 100 in the XnScore card. Finger taps the card -> XnScore
  Dashboard.
- **Narration:** "Your XnScore is your TandaXn track record on a
  zero to one hundred scale. It unlocks bigger circles, better advance
  terms, and lower insurance rates."
- **On-screen:** `XnScore -- your TandaXn track record`

## Scene 2 -- The breakdown (0:10 -- 0:38)

- **Scene:** XnScore Dashboard. Five bars / dial segments labelled
  with weights:
    Payment History 40%, Circle Completion 25%,
    Engagement 15%, Default Penalty 10%, Tenure 10%
- **Narration:** "Five factors drive it. Payment history is the
  heaviest -- on-time contributions matter most. Circle completion
  rewards finishing what you start. Engagement counts active days.
  Default penalty is the negative side. Tenure builds with time."
- **On-screen callouts:** `40% Payments`, `25% Completion`,
  `15% Engagement`, `10% Defaults`, `10% Tenure`

## Scene 3 -- The tiers (0:38 -- 0:55)

- **Scene:** Tier ladder graphic: Emerging (0-39), Building (40-59),
  Trusted (60-74), Established (75-89), Elder (90-100). The user's
  current tier (Trusted) is highlighted.
- **Narration:** "Five tiers from Emerging to Elder. Each tier unlocks
  more -- bigger advance caps, lower fees, larger circles you can
  join."
- **On-screen:** `Emerging -> Building -> Trusted -> Established -> Elder`

## Scene 4 -- The top opportunity (0:55 -- 1:18)

- **Scene:** "Top improvement" card surfaces: "Make 2 on-time
  contributions to gain +3 XnScore." Below it, a tap-target opens the
  upcoming contributions screen.
- **Narration:** "The Improvements card on your dashboard tells you
  the highest-value action you can take right now. Tap it to jump
  straight to the screen that resolves it."
- **On-screen:** `One tap to your fastest gain`

## Scene 5 -- The watchouts (1:18 -- 1:25)

- **Scene:** Brief flash of a "Score dropped" notification: "Late
  contribution: -2 XnScore."
- **Narration:** "Missed and late contributions cost points -- the
  app explains every change in your decision history."
- **On-screen:** `Every change is explained`

## End CTA (1:25 -- 1:25)

- **Scene:** End card.
- **On-screen:**
  ```
  Check your score today.
  Dashboard -> XnScore card.
  ```
