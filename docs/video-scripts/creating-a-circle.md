# Creating a Circle
**Runtime:** 75 seconds

## Scene 1: Hook (0:00 – 0:08)
**Visuals:** Cold open on a phone with TandaXn open. Hand taps the Circles tab in the bottom nav. Soft motion blur on the swipe.
**Narration:** "Saving with people you trust is the oldest financial tool there is. TandaXn calls them circles."
**On-screen:** `Circles. Save together.`

## Scene 2: Start the flow (0:08 – 0:20)
**Visuals:** Circles tab is open. A bright `+` floating action button pulses on the bottom-right; finger taps it. Screen transitions to Create Circle.
**Narration:** "Tap the plus button on your Circles tab. You'll pick a type, set the contribution, and invite people."
**On-screen:** `Step 1: Tap +`

## Scene 3: Pick a type (0:20 – 0:35)
**Visuals:** Circle Type picker shows five cards: Traditional, Goal, Emergency, Investment, Charity. Finger taps Traditional.
**Narration:** "Choose a type. Traditional rotates payouts evenly, Goal saves toward a shared target, Emergency builds a safety net. Pick the one that matches what you're saving for."
**On-screen:** `5 circle types`

## Scene 4: Set amount, members, frequency (0:35 – 0:55)
**Visuals:** Form with contribution amount slider at $100, member count stepper at 6, frequency dropdown set to Weekly. Live preview card updates: payout = $600 per cycle.
**Narration:** "Set the contribution each person pays per cycle, how many members will join, and how often. The preview shows the payout amount — everyone gets a turn."
**On-screen:** `$100 / week × 6 members = $600 payout`

## Scene 5: Invite (0:55 – 1:10)
**Visuals:** Invite screen — contacts list with checkmarks on a few names, plus a Share Link button. Finger taps Share Link; a system share sheet animates up.
**Narration:** "Invite people by phone, email, or a shareable link. Your circle goes active the moment everyone confirms."
**On-screen:** `Invite → Confirm → Start contributing`

## End Card
**Visuals:** TandaXn logo, App Store + Google Play badges below.
**Narration:** "Ready to start saving with people you trust? Download TandaXn and start today."
**On-screen:** `Open the Circles tab. Tap +.`
