## Long description (4000-char limit)

```
TandaXn is a community savings app built around one simple idea: money is
easier to save when you save together. It brings the age-old tradition of
tontines — also known as rotating savings and credit associations (ROSCA),
susu, sou-sou, or hui — into a modern app that anyone with a smartphone can
use.

── HOW IT WORKS ──

Create a savings circle, invite the people you trust, and agree on the
amount each member will contribute per cycle. Every cycle, the pot is paid
out to one member. When the last member is paid, everyone has received the
same amount back — but the discipline of saving with a group has replaced
the loneliness of saving alone.

TandaXn tracks every contribution, handles payouts automatically once each
cycle closes, and reminds members before deadlines so nobody misses a beat.

── WHO IT'S FOR ──

  • Diaspora communities pooling money to send back home
  • Groups of friends saving for a shared goal — travel, weddings, gifts,
    or the down payment on a first apartment
  • Small businesses building a cash buffer with trusted partners
  • Anyone who saves better with accountability than alone

── WHAT'S INSIDE ──

  • Savings circles — set the amount, the cycle, the members. TandaXn
    handles the rest.
  • Group trips — pool money and installments for a shared destination.
  • Dreams — a lightweight goal tracker for what you're saving for.
  • Community feed — share what your circle is up to, cheer people on.
  • Trust score — a private measure of your contribution history that
    unlocks larger circles and better terms over time.
  • Wallet — top up from your bank card, withdraw to your bank account,
    and see exactly where your money is at every step.
  • Real 2FA — sign in with a code from Google Authenticator, Authy, or
    any TOTP app.
  • Active session control — see every device signed in and revoke the
    ones you don't recognize.

── SECURITY AND PRIVACY ──

Your money and your data are handled with the same care a bank would.
Passwords are hashed and never stored in plain text. Payments are
processed by Stripe. Two-factor authentication is available for every
account. TandaXn never sells your data, and you can request full export
or deletion from your profile at any time.

── SUPPORT ──

Questions or feedback? Reach us at support@tandaxn.com. We reply within
one business day.

TandaXn is available in English and French. Additional languages are on
the roadmap.
```

**~2,750 / 4,000 chars** — room to add feature callouts without hitting the ceiling.
